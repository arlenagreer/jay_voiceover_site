About Jay

Hello! I'm Jay, a passionate voice actor based in Chicago. My journey into voice acting began with a love for storytelling and a background in theater. Over the past 5 years, I've had the privilege of lending my voice to hundreds of projects, ranging from national TV commercials to indie video games.

My voice is often described as warm, friendly, and trustworthy for commercials, while my character work ranges from zany creatures to gritty villains. I pride myself on my versatility and my ability to take direction well.

My Philosophy

I believe that every script has a soul, and it's my job to find it. Whether it's a 15-second ad or a 10-hour audiobook, I approach every project with the same level of dedication and professionalism. I'm not just reading words; I'm communicating your message.

Training & Experience

I've trained with some of the best coaches in the industry, focusing on commercial reads, character development, and improv. I'm constantly learning and refining my craft to stay current with industry trends.
